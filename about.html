About the Noa Art Studio

Maritza Noa is a distinguished Fine Artist, Educator, Illustrator and Digital Graphic Designer.  Mrs. Noa paints, illustrates educational children’s books, creates advertisement and corporate designs, besides teaching at the Noa Art Studio, founded and directed by her in 1991.

At the Studio she helps the students develop their creativity in several art disciplines and guides students through the assembling of their college portfolios.

She not only teaches but inspires her students to achieve remarkable careers in the arts like animation, industrial design, video games, architecture, fashion design, fine arts and many others.

Mrs Noa has touched many lives as they learned to present their artwork in exhibits and obtained noticeable nominations and honors in Puerto Rico and the United States.
